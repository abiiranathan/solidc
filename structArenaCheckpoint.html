Detailed Description

Lightweight save/restore token for O(1) allocation checkpointing.

Obtained via arena_save(); passed back to arena_restore() to roll back all allocations made since the snapshot. Blocks are never freed by restore — they remain committed and available for subsequent allocations.

Behaviour is undefined if the checkpoint is used after arena_destroy(), or with an arena other than the one that produced it.
